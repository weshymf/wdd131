São Paulo, Brazil

São Paulo is a Brazilian municipality, capital of the state of the same name and the main financial, corporate and commercial center in Latin America. It is the most populous city in Brazil, the American continent, Portuguese-speaking countries and the entire southern hemisphere, and the fifth most populous in the world, while its metropolitan region, with around 21 million inhabitants, is the seventh largest urban agglomeration on the planet. São Paulo is the most influential Brazilian city on the global stage, being, in 2016, the 11th most globalized city on the planet, receiving the classification of alpha global city, by the Globalization and World Cities Study Group & Network (GaWC). The city's motto, present in its official coat of arms, is Non ducor, duco, a Latin phrase that means "I am not led, I lead".
